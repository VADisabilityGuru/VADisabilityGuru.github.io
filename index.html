This is site's purpose is to help out every service member (Active or Reserves) Who need directions on what to expect with the VA disability benefits. This is the best site for service member's who are 1 year out from Retiring or separating. It is also for member's who have separated and have no idea how the VA disability system works. The resources in the site will help you be able to understand different things like submitting a claim, getting disabilities in your medical record, and what your first steps should be. You served this country, Now let this country serve you. ***** Disclaimer ***** This page is only for advice and resource. This site will not help you increase a claim or explain why a claim was denied. It is only meant for a highly detailed resource to help guide service member's who may not understand what the VA disability system is.
