This is site's purpose is to help out every service member (Active or Reserves) Who need directions on what to expect with the VA disability benefits. To begin, click the "Start" button above. Depending where you are at in your career, will determine where you should be at to prepare and then ultimately file a VA disability claim. Within this webpage are training videos, check-list(s), and even a medical avatar. As well, this page has all service connected disabilities and their codes. This site is design to bring clarity to services members who maybe lost in the process. If you have any questions or have a disability questions, please feel free to contact us below.


***** Disclaimer *****
This page is only for advice and resource. This site will not help you increase a claim or explain why a claim was denied. It is only meant for a highly detailed resource to help guide service member's who may not understand what the VA disability system is. Again, if you want more help or guidance, please click the "contact us" link below.
